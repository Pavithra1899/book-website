Newman and His Contemporaries

By Edward Short

"Newman and His Contemporaries" by Edward Short provides a compelling overview of Hollywood's golden age. Delving into the lives of Paul Newman, Marlon Brando, and James Dean, Short offers insights into their impact on cinema. Through vivid narratives, the book chronicles their rise as iconic figures, shaping the landscape of entertainment.
